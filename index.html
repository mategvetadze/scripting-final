About KIU

Kutaisi International University is a public research university established in 2020, designed to become an international hub of education, science, and technology in the Caucasus region.

Our Mission

Prepare highly qualified workforce and human capital that promotes economic growth in Georgia and the wider region.

Green Campus

160 hectares surrounded by forest with a river running through — only 25 km from Kutaisi International Airport.

International Focus

English-language programs, international faculty, and a multicultural environment welcoming students worldwide.
